HI UBER DRIVERS,

When you are driving around NYC and finding passengers, it might be possible that you will not find a single rider during your trip, and just waste oil and money time after time. NOW this website will help you to find the best time and location to get your passenger as quickly as possible.


Analyzing 1028136 Uber trips in September, 2014, we clearly find that the Uber passengers have a use pattern with strong regularity and repetition.


time

The best time to drive the carBelow, a line chart tracks the number of pickups of Uber occurring by hour over the course of the day. The two summits indicate the morning and evening rush hours (around 7 am and 6 pm). The number of ridership in evening rush hours is approximately twice the number of ridership in morning rush hour. This result is indicative to show the commuting trends. For instance, workers are more likely to take the subway or other transit to their work, but they may take an Uber which has more flexibility than public transit after a hard day at work.

location

The Tree map below is an interactive graph. You can click on the rectangles to know the exact number of Uber trips in each borough in September, 2014. It demonstrates how the vast majority of Uber pickups (76%) took place within Manhattan. The other boroughs represent a very small fraction of Uber pickups. Only 15% of pickups occurred in Brooklyn, 8% in Queens, 1% in Bronx and less than 1% in Staten Island. Therefore, it would be more likely to find a passenger in Manhattan borough.
